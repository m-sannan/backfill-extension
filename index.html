Backfill

Sync your Zoho Cliq calls to Google Calendar — automatically.

Backfill is a Chrome extension that scans your Zoho Cliq call and meeting history for any date range, and syncs matching time blocks to Google Calendar — so your actual call time shows up on your calendar and nothing gets missed.

  • Scans Direct Calls and Meetings
  • Sync to any writable Google Calendar
  • Duplicate-safe — re-running is always safe
  • CSV export of any scan
