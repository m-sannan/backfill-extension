Backfill

A Chrome extension that syncs your Zoho Cliq calls to Google Calendar.

Backfill automatically scans your Zoho Cliq call and meeting history for any date range you choose, and creates matching events on your Google Calendar — so your actual call time shows up on your calendar and nothing gets missed.

What Backfill does

  • Scans your Zoho Cliq Direct Calls and Meetings history for a date range you pick
  • Creates calendar events for each call, with the correct time and duration
  • Syncs to any Google Calendar you choose to write to
  • Tracks what's already been synced locally, so re-running never creates duplicates
  • Lets you export any scan as a CSV file
