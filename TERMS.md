## What Backfill does
Backfill is a browser extension that reads your Zoho Cliq call history (from a Cliq tab you have open) and creates corresponding events in a Google Calendar you choose to sync to. See [PRIVACY.md](https://github.com/m-sannan/backfill-extension/blob/main/PRIVACY.md) for full data handling details.

## No warranty
Backfill is provided "as is," without warranty of any kind. It depends on Zoho Cliq's page structure to read call history; if Cliq changes its interface, scanning may stop working until the extension is updated. We make no guarantee of uptime, accuracy, or fitness for a particular purpose.

## Your responsibility
You are responsible for reviewing synced calendar events for accuracy before relying on them. Backfill does not modify or delete existing calendar events — it only creates new ones you explicitly trigger via the Sync action.
